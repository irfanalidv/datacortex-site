Cancellation and Refund Policy

At DataCortex IQ (OPC) PVT LTD ("we," "us," "our"), we provide high-quality services such as AI-powered solutions, machine learning, and cloud services. This policy outlines strict guidelines for cancellations and refunds to ensure transparency and clarity for all parties involved.

1. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations of services purchased from DataCortex are subject to the following conditions:

  • Cancellations can be requested within 7 days of the initial purchase, provided the project or service has not yet been initiated.
  • Once a project has been initiated, scoped, or any work has begun, cancellations will not be accepted and no refunds will be issued.
  • If a cancellation request is approved within the 7-day window and before the project is initiated, you will be eligible for a full refund.

2. Refund Policy

Refunds will only be issued under the following strict conditions:

  • No refunds will be provided once the project has been initiated, scoping documents have been signed, or work has begun.
  • If a service is cancelled within 7 days of purchase and before any work has started, a full refund will be processed.
  • All refund requests must be made by contacting our support team via email at contact@datacortex.in.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after the approval of your request.

3. Non-Refundable Services

The following services are non-refundable once initiated:

  • Any custom AI development, machine learning, or cloud integration services after work has started.
  • Consultation fees, strategy sessions, or scoping documents once delivered to the client.
  • Subscription services, such as ongoing support, if they have been used or accessed.

4. How to Initiate a Cancellation or Refund Request

To request a cancellation or refund, please follow these steps:

  • Send an email to contact@datacortex.in with your order details, including your name, purchase date, and a detailed explanation of the reason for your request.
  • Our customer support team will review your request and respond within 2-3 business days.

5. Processing Time

Once a refund is approved, please allow 7-10 business days for the amount to be credited to your original payment method.

6. Exceptions

In rare circumstances, we may consider exceptions to this policy. These will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis and are at the sole discretion of DataCortex.

If you have any further questions regarding our Cancellation and Refund Policy, please contact us at contact@datacortex.in.
