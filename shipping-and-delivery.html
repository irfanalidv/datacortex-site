Shipping and Delivery Policy

At DataCortex IQ (OPC) PVT LTD ("we," "us," "our"), we strive to deliver services promptly and ensure a seamless experience for our clients. Please read this Shipping and Delivery Policy carefully as it outlines important information about the delivery of our services and products.

1. Delivery Methods

All services provided by DataCortex are digital in nature and are delivered through electronic means. This may include:

  • Cloud-based platforms and integrations.
  • Email delivery of project files, reports, and deliverables.
  • Direct access to cloud environments for ongoing services such as AI models or machine learning tools.

2. Delivery Timeline

Our service delivery timelines vary depending on the scope of the project. Generally, you can expect the following timelines:

  • Initial Project Setup: 3-5 business days after purchase confirmation.
  • Full Project Delivery: Depending on the complexity, projects are delivered within 2-8 weeks after project initiation.
  • Subscription Services: Ongoing services such as cloud monitoring, support, and access to tools are provided immediately upon completion of initial setup.

These timelines are estimates and may vary based on project complexity and client requirements. We will keep you informed of any changes or delays in the delivery schedule.

3. Shipping Fees

Since all services are delivered electronically, there are no shipping fees associated with the delivery of our services or digital products.

4. Delivery Notifications

You will receive a confirmation email as soon as your service or product has been delivered. This may include details of:

  • How to access your service (e.g., links to cloud platforms or data access points).
  • Next steps and project milestones.
  • Any relevant credentials for access to the service environment.

5. Delayed or Failed Delivery

If you do not receive a service or digital product within the agreed-upon timeframe, or if there are technical issues accessing your deliverable, please contact us immediately at contact@datacortex.in.

We will promptly investigate the issue and ensure that your service or product is delivered as soon as possible.

6. Access to Cloud Services

For cloud-based services, access is provided through secure channels such as login credentials or API keys. These access details are sent directly to your email upon project completion or setup.

Ensure that all access credentials are kept secure. We are not responsible for any unauthorized access or misuse of your cloud environment after the credentials have been delivered.
